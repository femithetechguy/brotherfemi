My Testimony

On the 25th of June 2025, at HOWJ Atlanta I had a life-changing encounter with the Lord Jesus Christ. It was a moment of divine personal conviction, revelation and transformation that set me on a new path of faith and purpose - I had a strong nudge in my soul to search God afresh at all cost. Since then, I have been committed to living as a bond servant of Christ, seeking to know Him more deeply and to reflect His love and grace in all aspects of my life.


This website is a faith-inspired space to share my journey, connect with fellow believers, and encourage a Christ-centered life of love, humility, and purpose.


I look forward to hearing from you, sharing insights, and growing together in faith. May the grace and peace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you always.
